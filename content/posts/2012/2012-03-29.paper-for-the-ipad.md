<p>Beautiful new drawing/creative app only for the iPad, <a href="https://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/stat?id=6PFrOqNV4B8&offerid=146261&type=3&subid=0&tmpid=1826&RD_PARM1=http%253A%252F%252Fitunes.apple.com%252Fca%252Fapp%252Fpaper-by-fiftythree%252Fid506003812%253Fmt%253D8%2526uo%253D4%2526partnerId%253D30" target="itunes_store">Paper by FiftyThree - FiftyThree, Inc. (Free)</a>, with an interesting model for making money: free app and then you buy the tools you want to use through in app purchases.</p>
<p><a href="https://vimeo.com/37254322">Direct link to video</a></p>
<p>Each add-on sells for $1.99:</p>
<ul>
<li>A soft pencil/sketch tool</li>
<li>Marker/outline tool</li>
<li>Ink pen</li>
<li>Watercolors</li>
</ul>
<p>Or buy all of them for $7.99.</p>
